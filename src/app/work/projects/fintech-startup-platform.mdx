## Overview

Co-founded and led the technical development of a revolutionary fintech platform that transforms how small and medium enterprises access financial services. The platform combines AI-driven credit assessment, automated loan processing, and comprehensive financial analytics to provide SMEs with institutional-grade financial tools.

## Key Features

- **AI Credit Scoring Engine**: Developed machine learning models that analyze 200+ data points including cash flow patterns, industry benchmarks, and alternative data sources to provide accurate credit assessments in under 5 minutes.
- **Automated Lending Pipeline**: Built a fully automated loan origination system that processes applications, performs risk assessment, and disburses funds within 24 hours, compared to traditional 2-4 week timelines.
- **Financial Analytics Dashboard**: Created comprehensive business intelligence tools that provide real-time insights into cash flow, profitability, and growth opportunities using advanced data visualization.
- **API-First Architecture**: Designed a scalable microservices architecture that enables third-party integrations and white-label solutions for financial institutions.
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Implemented comprehensive compliance framework meeting EU financial regulations including GDPR, PSD2, and MiFID II requirements.

## Technologies Used

- **Next.js & React**: Modern frontend with server-side rendering for optimal performance
- **Node.js & Express**: Scalable backend API with microservices architecture
- **Python & Scikit-learn**: Machine learning models for credit scoring and risk assessment
- **PostgreSQL & Redis**: Data persistence and caching for high-performance operations
- **AWS Cloud**: Production infrastructure with auto-scaling and disaster recovery
- **Docker & Kubernetes**: Containerized deployment and orchestration
